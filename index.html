Only the best beans

"As with some Bordeaux chateaux or certain Burgundy domains, the harvests from the best coffee farms are sold in advance, allocated by roaster. The producers selected their roaster customers based on their capacity to best add value to the raw material and to sell it at a fair price. In order to be able to travel to their customers' premises and to thus grasp how they work and better define their market, some farmers now integrate their travel expenses into the price of their coffees. For the subsequent harvests they can thus take into consideration the consumption habits in the concerned countries." - CEO
